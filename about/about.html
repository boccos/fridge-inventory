About Fridge Inventory

Fridge Inventory helps you turn the ingredients in your fridge into quick and creative meal ideas. Using the power of GPT, you can enter whatever ingredients you have, and FridgeFriend will suggest recipes tailored to your own kitchen.

Our Mission

To empower everyone to make the most of their ingredients, making meals with ingredients one may possibly not know what to do with, and to enjoy cooking!
